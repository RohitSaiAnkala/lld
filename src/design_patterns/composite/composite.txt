. It is a structural design pattern
. Used when tree like structure objects are required
    . a building has floors, a floor has rooms (room is the leaf)
    . a folder may have file or folders, and file is the leaf
. We will have an interface, both our Composite and Leaf classes will implement the interface.
. Easy to add new type of components, in the hierarchy.
. Code is reusable and its the best way to support hierarchial representations.
